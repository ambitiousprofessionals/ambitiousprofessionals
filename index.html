The Founder

Meet the man behind the mission

Abanindra Sahu

M.Com(Law) & CS Executive · Founder & Proprietor

Abanindra Sahu founded Ambitious Professionals in October 2018 with a clear mission: deliver quality, concept-driven learning in commerce and professional education.

With over 8 years of dedicated teaching experience since founding the institute in 2018, he specialises in Corporate Law and Auditing, mentoring students across multiple academic and professional levels. His classrooms blend academic rigour with real-world insight — turning difficult topics into clear, workable concepts, and helping students build genuine professional confidence, not just exam recall.
